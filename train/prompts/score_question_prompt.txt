What is your overall rating on the quality of this slide?
The rating should be a float between 1 and 10, rounded to two decimal places.

Please assess:

- Use of images, charts, and visual elements

Scoring Rules:

5.00-6.99 - Adequate quality with some issues but generally functional and understandable
7.00-8.99 - Good overall quality with minor issues, well-designed and clearly communicates information
